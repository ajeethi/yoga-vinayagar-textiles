Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Yoga Vinayagar Textiles ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order, contact us, or subscribe to our newsletter, we may collect:

  • •Your name, phone number, and email address
  • •Delivery address and order details
  • •Payment transaction references (we do NOT store card numbers)
  • •WhatsApp chat history for orders placed via WhatsApp

2. How We Use Your Information

  • ✓To process and fulfil your orders
  • ✓To send order updates, delivery notifications, and invoices
  • ✓To respond to your enquiries and provide customer support
  • ✓To send promotional offers (only if you've opted in)

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • •Courier/logistics partners to deliver your orders
  • •Payment processors (Razorpay/UPI) to process transactions securely
  • •When required by law or government authority

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) for all data transmissions. Your payment details are processed through secure payment gateways and never stored on our servers.

5. Cookies

We use browser localStorage to save your shopping cart and wishlist preferences. No third-party advertising cookies are used.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. Contact us at 89396 05447 to exercise these rights.
