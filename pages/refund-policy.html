Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: June 2025

🔄

Our Promise

We want you to be completely happy with your purchase. If you're not satisfied for any reason, we'll do our best to make it right.

Return Eligibility

You can return a product within 7 days of delivery if it meets the following conditions:

  • ✓Item is unused, unwashed, and undamaged
  • ✓Original tags and labels are intact
  • ✓Item is in its original packaging
  • ✓Return is initiated within 7 days of delivery date

Non-Returnable Items

  • ✕Sale / discounted items (unless defective)
  • ✕Blouse pieces and stitched items
  • ✕Customised or made-to-order items
  • ✕Items reported after 7 days of delivery

How to Return

  1. 1
    Contact Us

    WhatsApp or call 89396 05447 within 7 days of delivery with your order number and photos of the item.

  2. 2
    Approval

    We'll review your request within 24 hours and send you return approval with instructions.

  3. 3
    Ship the Item

    Pack securely and ship to our Madurai address. Return shipping cost is borne by the customer (unless the item was defective/wrong).

  4. 4
    Refund

    After inspection, refund is processed in 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

Damaged / Wrong Item

If you received a damaged or wrong product, we will arrange a free replacement or full refund — including return shipping. Please report within 48 hours of delivery with photos.

Contact us at 89396 05447 or 96776 05447 — we'll resolve it quickly and happily.
