👗 Products & Sizing

We stock a wide range: Cotton Sarees (handloom, Chettinad, printed), Silk Sarees (Kanchipuram, Banarasi, pure silk), Fancy Sarees (georgette, chiffon, net), Wedding Sarees, Designer Sarees, Stone Work Sarees, plus Kurtis, Tops, Nighties, Men's wear, and Kids' collections.
All our sarees are standard 5.5 metres with 0.8 metre blouse piece (total 6.3m). Some premium sarees include 1.2m blouse piece. Specific measurements are mentioned on each product listing.
Cotton Sarees: Hand wash in cold water with mild detergent. Air dry in shade. Silk Sarees: Dry clean only for best results. Store wrapped in a soft cloth. Fancy/Georgette: Gentle machine wash or hand wash. Avoid wringing. Iron on low heat from the reverse side.
