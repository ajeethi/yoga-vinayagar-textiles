✦ Who We Are

The Yoga Vinayagar Story

Yoga Vinayagar Textiles is a trusted textile showroom located in the heart of Madurai, Tamil Nadu. Situated at the New SMR Complex on Muthupatti Main Road, our showroom has been a go-to destination for customers seeking premium sarees, traditional textiles, and family garments.

We offer an extensive range of cotton sarees, silk sarees, wedding sarees, designer sarees, fancy sarees, chiffon sarees, stone sarees, kurtis, tops, nightwear, men's wear, and kids' collections — all handpicked for quality and value.

Whether you're a retail customer looking for the perfect saree for a special occasion, or a wholesale buyer stocking your boutique, we serve you with the same dedication — honest pricing, fresh designs every week, and genuine quality you can trust.

What Makes Us Different

Uncompromising Quality

Every piece in our collection is handpicked and quality-checked. We source directly from weavers and trusted mills to ensure you get authentic, durable textiles.

Fair & Transparent Pricing

We believe everyone deserves quality textiles at honest prices. Our factory-direct sourcing means you get premium fabric without the premium markup.

Customer-First Approach

Your satisfaction is our mission. From helping you choose the right saree to resolving any post-purchase concern — we are always here for you.

Fast Pan India Delivery

We deliver to every corner of India with trusted courier partners. Free shipping on orders above ₹999, with real-time tracking on every order.

Wholesale Friendly

We welcome boutique owners, retailers, and bulk buyers. Competitive wholesale pricing, flexible minimum orders, and dedicated support for business customers.

New Designs Weekly

We refresh our collection every week with the latest trends from Tamil Nadu's textile hubs. There is always something new to discover at Yoga Vinayagar Textiles.
