✦ Our Story

Madurai's Trusted Textile Destination

Yoga Vinayagar Textiles is a trusted textile showroom located in the heart of Madurai, Tamil Nadu. We offer an extensive range of premium sarees, cotton sarees, silk sarees, fancy sarees, kurtis, tops, nightwear and family garments — catering to retail customers and wholesale buyers alike.

Our showroom at the New SMR Complex, Muthupatti Main Road, stocks over 1000 hand-curated designs updated every week. We believe in quality fabrics, honest pricing, the latest designs, and outstanding customer service — with convenient online ordering and fast delivery across India.
